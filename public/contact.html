A Direct Line

I read every message that comes through this page.

That does not mean I can respond to all of them immediately, or at length, but it does mean your words will be received by a human being, not filtered into irrelevance.

If you are reaching out with sincerity, you are welcome here.

Reasons to Reach Out

You may wish to write if:

  • Something in the work resonated deeply and you want to share that experience
  • You have a thoughtful question related to the themes explored in the books or journal
  • You are inquiring about interviews, collaborations, or media requests
  • You are reaching out with a professional inquiry related to my writing

This space is not designed for noise or promotion. It is meant for considered communication.

How to Write

When you send a message, clarity matters more than length.

Say what you need to say plainly. I do not require flattery, explanation, or performance. If your message is genuine, that will be evident.

What to Expect

Responses may take time. I prioritize depth over speed, both in writing and in correspondence.

If your message requires a reply, I will respond when I am able. If it does not, know that it was still read with care.
