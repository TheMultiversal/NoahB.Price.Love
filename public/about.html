About Noah B. Price

A Brief Introduction

I am not interested in writing
what is easy to consume.

I am interested in writing
what lasts.

My work is shaped
by personal collapse and reconstruction,
long periods of study and reflection,
and an unwillingness to accept
inherited narratives
without scrutiny.

I write for readers
who are willing
to slow down, think carefully,
and feel fully.
