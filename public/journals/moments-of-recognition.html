Moments of Recognition

December 10, 2025

Writing mirrors life.

In moments of recognition,
the page reflects the soul,
capturing truths
that lived experience has etched.

Words become windows,
revealing patterns,
illuminating connections,
echoing the depths within.

Through writing,
we see ourselves anew,
recognizing the familiar
in the act of creation.

Moments of recognition
are the bridges
between inner and outer worlds.
