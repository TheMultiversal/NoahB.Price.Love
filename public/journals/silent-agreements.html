Silent Agreements

December 15, 2025

We make silent agreements.

Unspoken contracts
with ourselves,
promises whispered in the quiet,
commitments to paths unseen.

These agreements shape our days,
guide our choices,
define our boundaries.

They are the vows
we make in solitude,
the compacts that bind
our future selves.

In honoring them,
we find integrity,
in breaking them,
we discover grace.
