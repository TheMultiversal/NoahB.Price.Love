Unsettled Thoughts

December 25, 2025

Discomfort is a catalyst.

Unsettled thoughts
stir the soul,
challenging complacency,
inviting growth.

In the unease,
we confront our edges,
question assumptions,
embrace the unknown.

Growth demands discomfort,
the shedding of old skins,
the stretching into new forms.

Unsettled thoughts
are the seeds of transformation,
planting roots in fertile soil.
