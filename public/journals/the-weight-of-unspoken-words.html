The Weight of Unspoken Words

January 15, 2026

Silence has mass.

It exists in the space between breaths,
in the pause where meaning gathers before
it is forced into sound.

What is left unsaid
often presses harder on the mind than
anything that is announced.

In communication, words are but vessels,
carrying the freight of intent, but silence is the anchor,
grounding the exchange.

The power of silence
lies in its ability to listen,
to absorb, and to reflect back what is true.

It is the canvas
upon which understanding is painted,
the moment the painter steps back and sees
what was there all along.

In a world of noise,
silence is the profoundest speech.
