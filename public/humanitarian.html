The Ark Organization

Founded by Noah B. Price

The Mission

The Ark Organization was founded with one purpose:
to directly help families in need; not through
bureaucracy or committees, but through
immediate, personal action.

Over the past several years, hundreds of children
have been fed and families supported, often by
one person doing whatever it took,
even when the circumstances weren't ideal.

No salaries. No overhead. No profit.
Just people helping people.

The Work

What started as feeding children grew into
something larger, providing essentials,
offering stability, and showing families
that someone sees them.
